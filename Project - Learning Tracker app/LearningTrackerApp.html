Learning Tracker

A Learning curve is essential to growth!


It really is important in life to keep learning and gain knowledge. However, its also very important to keep track of your learning. It is the first step in understanding how you are managing your learning, and how you can increase or decrease your learning hours. It may seem like a lot of work when you first begin, but you can make it as simple or complicated as you want to. Tracking your Learning hours can help you in providing you an insight on how much more hours you need to spend learning to reach your goals.
